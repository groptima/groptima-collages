About Maya Devi University

Maya Devi University, established in 2010, operates under the Uttarakhand Private University (Amendment) Act 2024 and is approved by regulatory bodies including AICTE, UGC, PCI, and BCI. Located on a sprawling 15-acre campus in Selaqui, Dehradun, the university is committed to providing quality education with a focus on holistic development.

The university offers 171 courses across undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and doctoral levels, specializing in Paramedical Sciences, Engineering, Management, Law, Computer Applications, and Sciences. With state-of-the-art facilities and a commitment to environmental sustainability, Maya Devi University provides an ideal learning environment for students.
