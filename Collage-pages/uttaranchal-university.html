About Uttaranchal University

Uttaranchal University, established in 2013, is a prestigious private research university that holds the distinction of being the first university in Uttarakhand to achieve an "A+" Grade accreditation from NAAC in its first cycle. This remarkable achievement reflects the institution's commitment to academic excellence and quality education.

Recognized by UGC under Sections 2(f) and 12(B) and approved by AICTE, PCI, BCI, and AIU, the university offers over 100 programs across Engineering, Management, Law, Sciences, Pharmacy, Agriculture, Liberal Arts, Computer Applications, Nursing, Hotel & Hospitality Management, and Paramedical sciences. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and a focus on research and innovation, Uttaranchal University provides a world-class learning environment.
